# Genre-Specific Guidelines

Quick-reference table for genre parameters. Use this when producing a specific genre to get the right BPM, mode, sounds, and feel.

| Genre | BPM | Mode/Scale | Key Feel | Core Sounds | Signature Effect | Swing |
|-------|-----|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Lo-fi Hip Hop | 70-90 | Dorian/Major | Min7/Maj7 | Rhodes, soft drums, vinyl crackle | Low-pass everything, tape wobble | 0.55-0.60 |
| Synthwave | 100-120 | Minor | Minor | Saw pads, arp, punchy kick | Chorus, long reverb, sidechain | 0.50 |
| Trap | 130-170 (half) | Minor/Phrygian | Minor | 808 bass, hi-hat rolls, snare | Distorted 808, reverb snare | 0.50 |
| House | 120-130 | Dorian | Minor/Major | Off-beat chords, 4-on-floor kick | Sidechain pump, filter sweeps | 0.52-0.55 |
| Ambient | 60-80 | Lydian/Mixolydian | Modal/Sus | Pads, drones, granular texture | Long reverb (5s+), slow LFO | 0.50 |
| Trance | 130-145 | Minor | Minor | Supersaw, pluck arp, pad | Gated reverb, sidechain, riser | 0.50 |
| Drum & Bass | 170-180 | Minor | Minor | Reese bass, breakbeat, pad | Heavy compression, long reverb | 0.50-0.52 |
| Jazz | 100-140 | Dorian/Mixolydian | 7th/9th | Piano, walking bass, brushes | Room reverb, warmth | 0.62-0.67 |
| Classical | 60-120 | Major/Minor | Major/Minor | Strings, woodwinds, piano | Hall reverb, dynamics | 0.50 |
| Metal | 120-200 | Phrygian/Minor | Minor | Distorted guitar, double kick, bass | Heavy distortion, tight compression | 0.50 |
| Chiptune/8-bit | 120-160 | Major/Minor | Major/Minor | Square, pulse, triangle, noise | Bitcrush, no reverb | 0.50 |
| R&B | 60-80 | Dorian/Minor | Min7/9th | Smooth bass, keys, soft drums | Chorus, plate reverb | 0.58-0.62 |
| Reggae/Dub | 60-90 | Minor/Major | Minor/Major | Off-beat chords, deep bass, rim | Dub delay, spring reverb | 0.55-0.58 |
| Phonk | 130-140 (half) | Minor | Minor | Cowbell, distorted 808, Memphis chops | Tape saturation, heavy low-pass | 0.50 |
| Post-Rock | 100-130 | Major/Lydian | Major | Clean guitar, strings, crescendo | Long reverb, delay, tremolo | 0.50 |
| Funk | 100-120 | Dorian/Mixolydian | Min7/Dom7 | Slap bass, clavinet, tight drums | Tight compression, wah filter | 0.58-0.62 |
| Blues | 70-120 | Blues/Pentatonic | Dom7 | Guitar, piano, walking bass | Warm overdrive, room reverb | 0.67-0.75 |

## Using This Table

- Match BPM and swing first -- these define the genre's feel more than anything
- Mode/scale determines harmonic color; key feel determines chord voicing style
- Core sounds tell you which instrument builders to prioritize
- Signature effects are what make the genre recognizable -- always include them
- For genres not listed, research the closest match and adjust
