# Platform Specs & Character Limits

Single source of truth for platform constraints. All copy skills reference this file for character limits, above-fold thresholds, and formatting rules.

## Quick Reference Table

| Platform | Title/Caption Limit | Above the Fold | Optimal Length | Hashtag Count | Hashtag Placement |
|----------|-------------------|----------------|----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| YouTube | 100 chars | ~60 chars (search), ~40-50 (mobile) | 40-70 chars | 3-5 (max 15) | Description (long-form), Title (Shorts) |
| Instagram | 2,200 chars | ~125 chars | Mix short/long | 3-5 (max 30) | End of caption or first comment |
| TikTok | 4,000 chars | ~150 chars | Concise, keyword-rich | 3-5 (up to 9) | Inline or end of caption |
| Twitter/X | 280 chars (25K Premium) | ~125 chars | 70-100 chars | 1-2 | Mid-tweet or end, never first |
| LinkedIn | 3,000 chars | ~110-140 chars | 150-300 words | 3-5 | Bottom of post |

## YouTube

### Title
- Max: 100 characters (YouTube indexes all 100 for ranking)
- Display truncation: ~60 characters in desktop search results
- Mobile truncation: ~40-50 characters visible
- Sweet spot: 40-70 characters for the core hook
- Rule: Put most compelling element in first 50 characters
- SEO: First 3-5 words carry highest algorithmic weight

### Description
- Max: 5,000 characters
- Above the fold: First 150-200 characters visible before "Show more"
- Optimal length: 200-300 words (1,000-2,000 characters)
- First 2 lines: Hook + primary keyword + optional critical link/CTA
- Timestamps: Must start `00:00`, minimum 3, ascending, keyword-rich chapter titles
- Hashtags: 3-5 recommended, first 3 appear above video title. Max 15 (exceeding ignores ALL)

### Shorts
- Hashtags go in the title, after main title text
- Cap title hashtags at 2, use remaining in description

## Instagram

### Caption
- Max: 2,200 characters
- Above the fold: ~125 characters before "...more"
- Strategy: Mix short (60%, <150 chars), medium (30%, 150-500), long (10%, 500+)
- Formatting: Line breaks, emojis as bullets, short paragraphs
- SEO shift: Instagram now uses keyword search — integrate keywords naturally, not just hashtags
- Hashtags: 3-5 recommended (trending toward 5 hard cap). End of caption preferred.
- No clickable links in captions — direct to "link in bio"

### Reels
- Caption visible as overlay — keep it short and punchy
- Hashtags still matter for discoverability

## TikTok

### Caption
- Max: 4,000 characters
- Above the fold: Must tap "see more" — front-load compelling content
- SEO importance: TikTok is increasingly used as a search engine
- Keyword alignment: Hashtags should match words spoken in audio and written in caption
- Hashtags: 3-5 strategic (up to 9 with 3-3-3 strategy). Focus on relevance over quantity.
- No clickable links for most users — direct to bio link

## Twitter/X

### Post
- Max: 280 characters (standard), 25,000 (Premium)
- Optimal engagement: 70-100 characters
- URLs: Always count as exactly 23 characters regardless of actual length
- Hashtags: 1-2 maximum. More reduces engagement by 17%.
  - Never start a tweet with a hashtag
  - Mid-tweet placement works best
  - End-of-tweet is acceptable
- Threads: Strong hook, 4-8 tweets, numbered (1/), end with CTA + summary

## LinkedIn

### Post
- Max: 3,000 characters
- Above the fold: ~110 characters on mobile, ~140 on desktop before "See more"
- Optimal length: 150-300 words (800-1,600 characters)
- Formatting: Heavy use of line breaks, single-sentence paragraphs, whitespace
- Hashtags: 3-5 at bottom. PascalCase mandatory.
- Links: Posts with links may get slightly lower reach — consider putting links in first comment
- Tone: Professional, thought-leadership oriented

## Above-the-Fold Summary

The "above the fold" is the most critical real estate across ALL platforms — it's the only text guaranteed to be seen without user action:

| Platform | Above-Fold Chars | What Goes Here |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| YouTube Title | 50-60 | Hook + primary keyword |
| YouTube Description | 150-200 | Value proposition + primary keyword |
| Instagram | 125 | Hook line — must stop the scroll |
| TikTok | ~150 | Front-loaded compelling content |
| Twitter/X | 280 (full post) | Entire message visible |
| LinkedIn | 110-140 | Hook — contrarian, story, or benefit |
